High details of the racetracks in the new trailer of Gran Turismo 7

PlayStation explained in a video that Gran Turismo 7 has taken the series to a new level of realism in racetrack design. The official PlayStation Twitter page wrote: "Gran Turismo 7 takes this collection to a whole new level of realism. "Weather conditions and lighting at any time can dramatically change the driving experience on any track." The new Gran Turismo 7 teaser again shows parts of the overall experience presented in it. But as we look at the game's intriguing graphic effects, we hear Yamauchi Casunori talk about how Polyphony Digital Studios completely scanned every car track in the game in the real world; With a high-precision laser scanner that can record track details down to the smallest detail.
